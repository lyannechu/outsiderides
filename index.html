I Have a Ride! About OutsideRides

If you DON'T HAVE a ride:

  1. Click on map to see where others near you are going
  2. If they are heading near your destination and have empty seats, give them a call!
If you HAVE a ride:
  1. Click on "I have a ride!"
  2. Enter your destination and number of seats you have available
  3. Hit submit and a pop up will ask for your phone number so a user can contact you
  4. Go back to the home page and you will notice a pin has popped up in your current location
  5. Click on remove (on I have a ride page) to remove your pin
